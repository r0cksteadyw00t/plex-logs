### Mission 001: ScarFLIX - An Ambitious Technical Plan

#### Introduction
ScarFLIX is a cutting-edge streaming platform designed to disrupt the traditional media landscape by offering users unparalleled flexibility, customization, and control over their video content. The project aims to revolutionize the way people consume media by leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as AI-driven recommendations, virtual reality experiences, and blockchain-based currencies.

#### Objective
ScarFLIX's objective is to become a leading platform in streaming services, offering an immersive, personalized experience for users. The key objectives of this Mission are:

1. **Media Workflow Design**: Develop a robust media workflow that integrates with Plex, allowing seamless access and management of content.
2. **Catalogue Expansion Candidate Generation**: Identify potential expansion opportunities for the catalogue based on user preferences, genre trends, and emerging technologies.
3. **Plex Read-Only Analysis**: Conduct thorough analysis of existing Plex databases to identify unused or underutilized content, potentially expanding the library by 50%.
4. **Dry-Run Test Gates**: Implement a rigorous testing process that ensures the platform can handle high volumes of traffic without breaking down or affecting performance.
5. **Promotion Gates**: Develop a comprehensive promotion strategy to generate buzz and attract users to ScarFLIX.

#### Technology Stack
- **Plex**: Used for content management and streaming.

- **Blockchain**: For secure transactions, user authentication, and content distribution.
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: For scalable hosting of the platform.
- **Docker/Kubernetes**: For containerization and deployment automation.
